Course Description

In this course, we will be looking at questions of shape, size, relative position of figures, and the properties of shape. Geometry has different subfields, but we are going to focus on Euclidean geometry.

Euclid was a Greek mathematician who is referred to as the father of geometry. Euclidean geometry is the classical sense of geometry which essentially studies the fundamentals of points, lines, angles, shapes, etc.
